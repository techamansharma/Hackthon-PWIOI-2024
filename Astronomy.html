Fun Facts About Space

Fact 1: The Sun is Huge!

Did you know? The Sun is so big that 1.3 million Earths could fit inside it!

Fact 2: The Moon's Gravity

The Moon's gravity is only one-sixth that of Earth's. If you weigh 60 kg on Earth, you'd weigh just 10 kg on the Moon!

Fact 3: There Are Billions of Galaxies

Our galaxy, the Milky Way, is just one of billions of galaxies in the universe!
