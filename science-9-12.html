What is Science?

Science is the exploration of the natural world. It helps us understand the mysteries of life, the universe, and everything in between. Scientists use observations, experiments, and evidence to explain how things work. Whether it's studying planets far away or learning how plants grow, science is all around us!

Branches of Science

Science is divided into many branches. Each branch studies a different aspect of the world we live in. Some of the major branches include:

  • Physics: The study of matter, energy, and the forces that govern the universe.
  • Chemistry: The study of substances and how they interact, combine, and change.
  • Biology: The study of living organisms and their environments.
  • Earth Science: The study of the Earth, including its atmosphere, oceans, and geology.
  • Astronomy: The study of the stars, planets, and the universe beyond Earth.
